Ingredients:
- 3/4 Cups Flour (see below)
- 1 egg
- ~3/4 tsp salt (or to taste)
- ~1 tbsp water (depending on flour and humidity) 

Directions:
  1. Mix the dry ingredients, make a well in the center, and beat the eggs and water inside.
     Then slowly combine the ingredients together. 
  2. Knead that dough.
  3. The dough needs to rest before we stretch it, otherwise it will not make nice thin noodles.
  5. Stretch it
  6. Cut the dough!
  7. Boil it
  8. Add some soup and eat.
